ABOUT US

Organic spices are spices that are grown, processed, and certified
according to organic standards. These standards vary slightly from country to country but generally involve practices that promote soil health,
biodiversity, and ecological balance, while prohibiting the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, and genetically modified organisms (GMOs).
